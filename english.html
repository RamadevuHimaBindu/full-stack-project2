Tenses

Tenses are used to indicate the time of action. Three major types: Present, Past, and Future. Each has 4 sub-types.

Example: She is writing a letter. (Present Continuous)

Prepositions

Prepositions show the relationship of a noun/pronoun with another word. Common ones: in, on, at, under, over, between.

Example: The book is on the table.
